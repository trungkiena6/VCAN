硬件
=======================


**单片机** ：STM32F107VCT6

	* F = general-purpose
	* 107 = connectivity, USB OTG FS & Ethernet
	* V = 100 pins
	* C = 256 Kbytes of Flash memory
	* T = LQFP
	* 6 = Industrial temperature range, –40 to 85 °C.
	* 64k RAM
	* 5 × USARTs,
	* 4 × 16-bit timers,
	* 2 × basic timers,
	* 3 × SPIs,
	* 2 × I2S,
	* 1 × I2C,
	* USB OTG FS,
	* 2 × CANs,
	* 1 × PWM timer,
	* 2 × ADCs,
	* 2 × DACs,
	* Ethernet
	* CPU frequency 72 MHz

**CAN收发器** ：PCA82C251T
